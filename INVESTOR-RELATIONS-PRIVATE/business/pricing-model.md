## Revenue Model Breakdown

### Revenue Streams

1. **Subscription Revenue (80%)**
   - Monthly/annual subscriptions
   - Predictable, recurring revenue
   - High gross margin (~80%)

2. **Overage Fees (15%)**
   - Usage beyond plan limits
   - Scales with customer growth
   - High gross margin (~90%)

3. **Enterprise Contracts (5%)**
   - Custom pricing and SLAs
   - Higher ACV, longer contracts
   - High gross margin (~85%)

### Unit Economics

**Average Customer:**
- **ACV:** $600/year ($50/month average)
- **CAC:** $300 (6 months payback)
- **LTV:** $3,600 (3-year average, 5% monthly churn)
- **LTV:CAC:** 12x
- **Gross Margin:** 80%

## FAQ

**Q: What counts as a reconciliation?**  
A: One reconciliation = matching one source transaction with one target transaction.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my plan limit?**  
A: You're automatically charged $0.01 per reconciliation over your limit. No service interruption.

**Q: Can I upgrade/downgrade anytime?**  
A: Yes! Upgrades are immediate. Downgrades take effect at the end of your billing cycle.

**Q: Do you offer discounts for annual billing?**  
A: Yes! Save 17% with annual billing (equivalent to 2 months free).

**Q: What's included in support?**  
A: Email support with SLA based on your plan. Enterprise includes dedicated account manager.

**Q: Can I get a custom plan?**  
A: Yes! Contact sales@settler.io for custom pricing based on your needs.
