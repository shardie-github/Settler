## Executive Summary

This document outlines a 30-day trial cadence with strategic email and in-app touchpoints designed to:

1. Activate users quickly (Day 0-2)
2. Demonstrate value (Day 2-7)
3. Introduce gated features (Day 7-14)
4. Showcase success stories (Day 14-21)
5. Create urgency and drive conversion (Day 21-30)

---

## Trial Structure

### Trial Length: 30 Days

### Activation Requirements:

- Email address (required)
- Basic firmographic info (optional but encouraged):
  - Company name
  - Industry
  - Monthly transaction volume
- **No credit card required** (recommended for higher conversion)

### Day 0 Experience:

**Welcome Dashboard Should Include:**

1. **Welcome Message**
   - "Welcome to your 30-day free trial!"
   - "Here's how to get value in the next 10 minutes"

2. **Simple AI-Generated News Feed**
   - Industry-specific news/articles
   - Platform-specific tips
   - Best practices

3. **Quick Start Options**
   - "Connect your first platform" (Shopify, Stripe, etc.)
   - "Upload a sample campaign" (for email analysis)
   - "Try a demo reconciliation" (pre-configured example)

4. **Pre-Test Prompt** (Optional but encouraged)
   - "Help us personalize your experience"
   - 5-6 quick questions
   - Can be dismissed, but persistent banner until completed

5. **Clear Value Proposition**
   - "What you get free" section:
     - Basic AI news feed
     - 3 playground runs per day
     - Basic email campaign analysis
     - Access to basic cookbooks
   - "What you unlock on paid" section:
     - Personalized feed based on pre-test
     - Unlimited playground runs
     - Advanced email insights
     - Full cookbook library
     - Free 30-minute consultation
     - Priority support

## A/B Testing Recommendations

### Test Variables:

1. **Trial Length**
   - 14 days vs 30 days
   - Measure conversion rate

2. **Credit Card Requirement**
   - No CC vs CC required
   - Measure signup rate and conversion

3. **Consulting Timing**
   - Day 2-3 vs Day 7
   - Measure engagement and conversion

4. **Urgency Messaging**
   - High urgency vs low urgency
   - Measure conversion in final days

5. **Email Frequency**
   - Daily (final 3 days) vs Every other day
   - Measure engagement and unsubscribes

## Success Metrics

### Track These KPIs:

1. **Activation Rate**
   - % of trial users who complete pre-test
   - % who connect first platform
   - % who run first reconciliation

2. **Engagement Rate**
   - Daily active users during trial
   - Average sessions per user
   - Feature usage (which features used)

3. **Conversion Rate**
   - Trial ‚Üí Paid conversion %
   - Time to conversion (days)
   - Conversion by touchpoint (which email/CTA converted)

4. **Retention Rate**
   - % who remain active after trial
   - % who upgrade within 7 days of trial end
   - % who return after trial ends
